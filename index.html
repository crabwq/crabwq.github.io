Research Interests

Computer vision, pattern recognition, machine learning methods and their applications particularly in:

• Video surveillance, including target tracking, abnormal detection, crowd analysis, traffic sign detection, scene understanding, pedestrian detection, etc.

• Hyperspectral image analysis, including HSI classification, segmentation, band selection, abnormal detection, etc.

• Neural perception modeling, including visual saliency detection, color difference perception, etc.
